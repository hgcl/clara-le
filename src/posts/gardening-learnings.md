## NPK or the 3 essential nutrients

Nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K) are known as the three crucial nutrients for healthy plant growth.

- Nitrogen (N): for plant growth, and greening up plants — think UP.
- Phosphorus (P): for better root developement, and helping produce blooms — think DOWN.
- Potassium (K): promotes all around wellbeing, improves resistance to illnesses and droughts — think ALL AROUND.

Here is how to naturally get those nutrients:

- Nitrogen (N): [nettle slurry](/recipes/nettle-slurry/), coffee grounds
- Phosphorus (P): ground egg shells, wood ash[^1]
- Potassium (K): ground egg shells, banana peels, comfrey slurry[^2]

[^1]: Wood ash will make the soil more alkaline. It will be bad for plants that need an acidic environement (e.g. strawberry, raspberry, blackberry trees).

[^2]: In French: purin de consoude
