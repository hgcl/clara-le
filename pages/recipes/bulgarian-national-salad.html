Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 feta cheese, diced
  • 0.5 red bell pepper, dicedoptional
  • 1 small red onionoptional
  • 1 tbsp. sunflower oiloptional
  • 0.5 tbsp. red wine vinegaroptional
  • Fresh parsley, thinly choppedoptional
  • Pepper and saltoptional

Link to original recipe

Instructions

  1. Dice the cucumber, feta cheese, tomatoes and bell pepper. Dice the onion in small pieces. Add everything to a big salad bowl.
  2. Drizzle sunflower oil and vinegar over diced vegetables. Season with salt and pepper. Chop parsley and sprinkle on salad. Stir to coat evenly.

Note: It is possible to omit everything except for the cucumber, tomatoes and feta cheese.
